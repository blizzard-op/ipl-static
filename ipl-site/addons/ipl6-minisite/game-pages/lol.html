IPL6 is coming back to Sin City on 3.28 with a League of Legends tournament!

League of Legends Championship Series NA Matches

: As stated by Riot Games in all of their publications, March 28th & 29th’s weekly matches for LCS will take place on IPL’s stage for those matches. This allows fans from other regions than Los Angeles to attend the games live and meet the NA pros in person.

Challengers’ Cup

: The professionals that are in Season 3 of LCS won’t be the best forever as new challengers approach daily to fight for the coveted title as a Professional League of Legends competitor. These new challengers will get their first taste of real competition at IPL6 as they compete for prizes, recognition, and the first step to qualify for the second half of LCS Season 3.

International Exhibition

: Six (6) additional International LCS teams will converge on IPL6 in Las Vegas to offer fans a snapshot at how the competition in each region is progressing. These exhibitions will showcase the different flavors in strategy that develop out of each geographical region and each individual tournament.

IPL6 Tournament Structure

Fresh ideas will be brought forth to help you decide which teams you think are going to take down their opponents’ nexus and inspire millions of League of Legends fans around the world. All throughout the event there will be challenges and contests for those of you attending the event as well as anyone watching from home!
