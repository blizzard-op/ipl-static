StarCraft II returns to the IPL6 circuit bigger and better than ever!

There will be 3 events at IPL6, which is being held at the Cosmopolitan of Las Vegas, March 28th - March 31st:

  • Main Event - Wings of Liberty
  • IPTL 2013 Season 1 Finals
  • Heart of the Swarm Invitational

Wings of Liberty will be the main event for IPL6. The Wings of Liberty tournament is a 40 person tournament with both invite and open spots.

Heart of the Swarm: IPL will be holding an 8 person Heart of the Swarm Invitational at IPL6.

IPTL Finals

The finals of IPTL 2013 Season 1 will be played at IPL6 featuring the top 2 teams, still to be determined. Both teams will have six players flown out to the event, but are free to bring any additional players to the event with their own budget, or if they qualify to IPL6 through additional means.

Keep your eyes on ign.com/ipl for all the updates on IPL6 and make sure to join us in the Cosmopolitan of Las Vegas, the official home of the IPL!

Players

  • Leenock (IPL5 Champion)
  • viOLet (IPL5 Runner up)
  • Polt (IPL5 3rd place)
  • 12 Players from IPTL Finals Teams

To clarify, six players from each IPTL finals team will receive paid trips to IPL6 to compete in addition to playing the finals at the event.

For each Regional, the winner of the tournament will receive an invite and free travel and hotel for the event. The runner up for each regional will receive a spot in the event, but no travel.

For Satellite events, the winner receives a spot in the event, but no travel. For the International Satellite, the top 3 players all qualify for the event, but do not win travel.

You may only sign up for a Regional or Satellite if you live in that region OR hold citizenship in that region OR will be travelling from that region for IPL6. You may sign up for both a Regional and Satellite and as many as you are eligible for. You must pick ONE region to play in, and may not play in Regionals / Satellites from multiple regions.

Signups close for each qualifier 24 hours before the event begins.
