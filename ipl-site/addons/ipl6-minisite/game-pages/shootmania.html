IPL6 is coming back to Sin City on 3.28 with a ShootMania tournament!

We will be hosting a ShootMania tournament for IPL6 which is set to take place at the Cosmopolitan in Las Vegas on March 28th to March 31st! The format for the tournament will be a sixteen team double elimination bracket.  We will be holding five North American qualifiers and three European qualifiers for a grand total of ten North American teams and six European teams. These qualifiers will give seeding points to the top two teams, and the earlier you qualify the more seeding points you will be awarded. The qualifiers will be bo3 up until the semifinals where they will change to bo5. First to 9 points wins, and you must win by 2

We will also be hosting four North American and four European Weekly Cups that coincide with our qualifiers that will give seeding points to the top four teams.  There will also be a $150 prize to the first place team for each Cup.  The rules will be the same as the qualifiers, except it will be bo3 until the finals which are bo5.

Seeding points

  • Seeding points will determine the seeding for our IPL6 bracket. Here are the values of each tournament qualifier and weekly cup:
  • Qualifier 1 - 10 points to first place, 9 points to second place
  • Qualifier 2 - 9 points to first place, 8 points to second place
  • Qualifier 3 - 8 points to first place, 7 points to second place
  • Qualifier 4 (NA only) - 7 points to first place, 6 points to second place
  • Qualifier 5 (NA only) - 6 points to first place, 5 points to second place
  • All Weekly Cups - 3 points for first place, 2 points for second, and 1 point each for third and fourth
