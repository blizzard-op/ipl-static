Ticket Packages and Promotional Room Rates Now Available!

Ticket Sales:

Tickets are available for purchase here: http://www.cosmopolitanlasvegas.com/IPL5

VIP Tickets

VIP Tickets are $250 in advance and $300 at the door. A VIP ticket grants you reserved front row seating, priority admission to the event, exclusive access to a VIP player area, a $25 Cosmopolitan Gift Card, an IPL5 t-shirt, and a commemorative poster. VIP ticket holders are the only ticket holders to be able to check-in on Thursday, November 29th and have in person access to the qualifier events that day.

Also, if you buy your VIP tickets before September 6th, you will receive a $50 Cosmopolitan Gift Card instead of a $25 Cosmopolitan gift card!

General Admission Tickets

General Admission tickets cost $40 only if purchased before September 30th, $50 only between September 30th and the event, and $60 at the door. The General Admission ticket holders will gain access to check-in and the venue beginning on Friday, November 30th.

Gold Packages

$80 for IGN Gold Early Bird 3-Day (11/30 - 12/2) General Addmission Ticket & $50 The Cosmopolitan of Las Vegas Giftcard

$125 for IGN Gold Early Bird 3-Day (11/30 - 12/2) General Addmission Ticket & $100 The Cosmopolitan of Las Vegas Giftcard

Room Rates

IPL 5 room rates are finally here! Rates are valid starting from Wednesday 11/28 through Monday 12/3 (checkout). Rooms are $189 for Friday/Saturday, and $129 for the other days. The special room rates are available for booking until Monday 11/5: https://resweb.passkey.com/go/IPL

Gift card terms and usage

The tournament will begin on Thursday, November 29th with a special VIP access only day. Here, VIP ticket holders will be able to access the venue a day before anyone else, and there will even be a special get together with the pro players on Thursday night! Additionally, there will still be at least four HD streams covering the event for those of you at home.

General admission begins on Friday, November 30th and runs through December 2nd.
